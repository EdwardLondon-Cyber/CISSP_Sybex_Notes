= Business Continuity Planning

*_Business Contiuity Planning (BCP):_* Involves assessing the risk to organizational processes and creating policies, plans, and procedures to minimixe the impact those risks might have on the organization if they were to occur.

== Planning for Business Continuity

A Business Continuity Plan is used to maintain the continuous operation of a business in the event of the emergency

Business Continuity Plans vs Disaster Recovery Plans

* Business Continuity Plans ( Strategic Level/Business Processes)
* Disaster Recovery Plans (Tactical Level/Technical implementation)

*_The BCP process has four main steps_*

* Project scope and planning

* Business impact analysis

* Continuity Planning

* Approval and Implementation
